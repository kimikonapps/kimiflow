# Frontend Quality — Flagship Delta

Read after the Standard file only for explicit polish, redesign, UI/UX uplift or visual refresh work.

- Preserve the confirmed product goal and source truth while pushing hierarchy, composition, spacing, responsive behavior, state quality and interaction clarity to release-showcase quality.
- Inspect the whole visible composition, not isolated components. Remove accidental near-alignments, uneven visual weight, weak focal order, awkward wrapping and inconsistent density.
- Refine microcopy, focus/hover/pressed transitions and purposeful motion where they materially improve comprehension. Keep reduced-motion behavior and familiar affordances.
- Do not add novelty for its own sake. A quieter, more precise result is better than extra effects or architecture.
- Run a fresh final visual pass. Flagship closes P0/P1/P2 and every concrete, reversible P3 polish finding; purely subjective alternatives are not blockers.
